What is Bullying?

Bullying is when a person repeatedly uses their words or actions to hurt another person or group of people. This can take on many forms such as hitting or pushing someone for no apparent reason, calling others hurtful names, embarrassing someone in-front of others, and many more things that can cause harm to others. Bullying commonly happens in schools and online where people can remain anonymous and where information spreads faster than anywhere else. In cases of bullying, there are usually 3 types of people present. These people are the victims, the bullies, and the bystanders.
